# Community Guidelines - Lovendo

**Welcome to the Lovendo community!**

Lovendo is about authentic travel connections. To ensure a safe and enjoyable journey for everyone,
we have established these guidelines.

## 1. Respect & Kindness

- **Be Nice:** Harassment, bullying, or hate speech is strictly prohibited.
- **Consent:** Respect boundaries. No means no.
- **Inclusivity:** Discrimination based on race, gender, religion, or sexual orientation will result
  in an immediate ban.

## 2. Safety First

- **Real Verified People:** Do not impersonate others. Use our verification tools (iDenfy) to prove
  your identity.
- **No Financial Scams:** Asking for money, wire transfers, or gift cards outside of our official
  "Gift" system is a violation.
- **No Solicitation:** Prostitution, escort services, or human trafficking are illegal and strictly
  banned. We report such activities to law enforcement.

## 3. Authenticity

- **Original Photos:** Use clear photos of yourself. No fake, stolen, or celebrity photos.
- **Honest Bio:** Be truthful about your travel plans and interests.

## 4. Prohibited Content

- **Nudity/Sexual Content:** We are a travel dating app, not a porn site. No sexually explicit
  photos or bio content.
- **Violence:** No graphic violence or promotion of self-harm.
- **Illegal Acts:** No promotion of drug use or illegal acts.

## 5. Reporting Violations

If you see something that violates these rules:

1. Tap the **"Report"** icon on the user's profile.
2. Select the reason.
3. Our Trust & Safety team reviews all reports within 24 hours.

**Zero Tolerance Policy:** Violating these guidelines may lead to account suspension or permanent
ban without refund.
