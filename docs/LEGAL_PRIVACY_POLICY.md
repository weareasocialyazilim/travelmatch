# Privacy Policy & Data Deletion (Lovendo)

**Last Updated:** January 19, 2026

## 1. Data Collection

We collect the following personal data to provide the dating and travel matching service:

- **Identity:** Name, email, phone number, date of birth, gender.
- **Profile:** Photos, bio, interests, languages spoken.
- **Location:** Geolocation (if permitted) for matching with nearby travel partners.
- **Usage:** Messages, likes, swipes, and app interactions.
- **Device:** Push tokens, device usage for analytics (PostHog).

## 2. Data Usage

Your data is used solely for:

- Matching you with relevant travel partners.
- Facilitating secure messaging and video chats.
- Trust & Safety verification (Identity check).
- Sending push notifications for matches and messages.
- Improving our algorithms (anonymized data).

## 3. Data Deletion (Right to be Forgotten)

You have the full right to delete your account and all associated data at any time.

### How to Delete Your Account:

1. Open the Lovendo App.
2. Go to **Settings > Account**.
3. Tap **"Delete Account"**.
4. Confirm user deletion.

**What happens next?**

- Your profile is immediately hidden from other users.
- Your personal data (email, phone, photos) is permanently deleted or anonymized within 30 days
  (backup retention).
- Your messages are anonymized (sender name removed), but the content may persist in the recipient's
  inbox unless it contains PII.
- Analytics events are disassociated from your identity.

### Manual Deletion Request

You can also request deletion by emailing **privacy@lovendo.xyz** with the subject "Data Deletion
Request".

## 4. Third Party Sharing

We share minimal data with:

- **Supabase:** Database and Authentication hosting.
- **Cloudflare:** Image hosting and security.
- **PostHog:** Product analytics (anonymized where possible).
- **PayTR:** Payment processing (we do not store credit card info).
